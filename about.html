Good health starts on your plate — not in a pill.

At About Nutrition, we help you simplify food, understand your body, and take charge of your health — one real, balanced meal at a time.

Whether you're trying to reverse a chronic condition or just want to feel more energetic and in control, you're in the right place. We blend clinical nutrition science with practical Indian food habits to create sustainable, life-changing results.

I'm Seema Goel, a clinical nutritionist with over 25 years of experience helping people just like you — across geographies and generations — reclaim their health through food.

I've worked in hospitals, coached thousands of clients, trained nutrition professionals, and realized one thing: most people don't need another diet plan — they need clarity.

That's why About Nutrition was born.

To teach you how to understand your plate, make sense of your lab reports, and feel confident in the way you eat — not confused or guilty.
