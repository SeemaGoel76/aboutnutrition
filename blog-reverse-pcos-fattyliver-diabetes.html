🍽️ You Don’t Need to Quit Indian Food. You Need to Rewire It.

  • PCOS: Start your day with protein (not chai + biscuit), balance estrogen-clearing veggies with healthy fats, cut evening sugar crashes with seeds + soaked nuts, repair the gut — where most hormone chaos begins
  • Fatty Liver: Swap refined wheat for soaked rice, millet, and dal, eat before sunset — and stop grazing all day, add bitters (methi, karela, turmeric) naturally
  • Type 2 Diabetes: Build insulin-friendly thalis: dal + sabzi + salad, use sequencing: eat veg > protein > carb, cut late dinners and bedtime fruit habits
