Are There Any Limitations?

Yes. Science has its boundaries — and so do we. We're honest about:

When organs have been severely compromised

Genetic or mechanical defects

Conditions that are structural or inherited

Conditions that need emergency or intensive care

When immediate medical intervention is required

Still, even in these cases, our programs often improve quality of life, reduce medication load, and bring clarity.
